# Quiet, eco-friendly lawn and garden care — with standards we stand behind.

At EcoMow, we use battery-powered equipment charged by solar to deliver low-noise, sustainable lawn and garden services. This charter sets out what you can expect from us — every job, every suburb, across Canberra.

## Charter points

## Our Service Promise

- Friendly, professional team.
- Careful, consistent workmanship — no rushed jobs.
- Respect for your time, property and neighbours.

## Clear Quotes & Scope

- Itemised quotes that spell out what's included (e.g., mowing height, edging, hedge length/height, clippings handling, green-waste disposal).
- Any variations or extras are approved by you first — no surprise charges.
- If a deposit is needed for larger projects, we'll state that up front; otherwise payment is due on completion.

## Scheduling & Communication

- Confirmed booking window by SMS or email.
- If weather or access prevents work, we'll notify you promptly and propose the next available time.
- Optional photo updates of completed work on request.

## Workmanship Standards

- Lawn mowing: even cut, no scalping; edges trimmed; hard surfaces cleared.
- Hedging: neat lines, safe drop zone, tidy finish.
- Garden cleanups and weeding: beds left clear; paths and patios debris-free.
- Gutter cleaning: gutters cleared and downpipes checked (where safely accessible).
- Mulching: applied at agreed depth and away from trunks and stems.

## Environmental Care (Canberra-Smart)

- Battery-powered, solar-charged tools for lower noise and emissions.
- No blowing grass or debris into streets or stormwater drains — we collect, bin or mulch on site as agreed.
- Sensible herbicide use only when needed and by request; otherwise we prioritise manual and low-impact methods.

## Noise & Neighbours

- We operate within Canberra's permitted daytime noise and activity hours.
- Our battery equipment keeps sound levels down; if you have shift workers, babies or other sensitivities, tell us and we'll schedule accordingly.

## Safety, Scope & Powerlines

- We only undertake tasks we are properly equipped and trained to perform.
- Near live powerlines: we do not perform tree or pruning work in the hazard zone; we'll refer you to an Evoenergy-accredited arborist.
- Ladders and roof work are conducted only where it's safe to do so.

## Property Care & Access

- We take care with gates, fences, irrigation and garden ornaments.
- Please secure pets and provide access to power and water (if needed).
- If we accidentally damage something, we'll own it and fix it.

## Payment & Invoicing

- Simple, itemised invoices with the same price as quoted (unless you approved a variation).
- Common payment options available; receipts provided.
- For regular maintenance, you can choose per-visit or monthly billing.

## Cancellations & No-Shows

- If you need to cancel or change a booking, please give 24 hours' notice.
- If we're ever running late or held up by weather, we'll keep you updated and reschedule promptly.

## Our Guarantee & Your Rights

- Work is delivered with due care and skill and within a reasonable time. If we miss the mark, we'll return to make it right.
- Your consumer rights under the Australian Consumer Law apply in addition to this charter.

## Feedback & Complaints

- Tell us straight away — most issues are fixed same or next visit.
- If you're unhappy after we've tried to resolve it, you can contact Access Canberra (Fair Trading) for advice. We'll cooperate fully to reach a fair outcome.

## Privacy

- We only use your contact details for quoting, scheduling and service updates.
- We won't share your details with third parties without your consent.

## Service Area & Hours

- We serve all of Canberra (Inner North, Inner South, Belconnen, Gungahlin, Woden Valley, Weston Creek, Tuggeranong and Molonglo Valley).
- Booking windows are during permitted daytime operating hours in the ACT.
