How we do it

Scheduled maintenance that works with property management.

Commercial properties, strata complexes, body corporate common areas, and managed rental properties all need reliable, professional garden maintenance on a set schedule. Weekly, fortnightly, or monthly — we fit into whatever routine your property management requires.

Scheduled visits cover standard services: mowing common lawn areas, edging, hedging, weeding garden beds, and blowing paths clear. We can tailor the work to your property's specific needs. We notice maintenance issues — broken irrigation, plants struggling, areas that need attention — and let you know so you can plan ahead.

Battery-powered equipment is a genuine advantage for commercial properties. No petrol fumes near building air intakes or in enclosed courtyards. Quiet enough to run during business hours without disrupting office workers or residents. It's a professional, eco-friendly approach that properties can promote as part of their sustainability commitments.

Kai provides professional invoicing, works directly with property managers and body corporate committees, and understands the formal approval and payment processes that come with managing multi-unit properties. We're reliable, consistent, and easy to work with.

FAQs

Common questions about commercial and strata maintenance.

Do you provide regular maintenance for body corporate properties?

Yes. We provide scheduled maintenance for body corporate common areas, strata complexes, and residential complexes. Whether it's weekly, fortnightly, or monthly visits, we handle mowing, edging, hedging, weeding, and path blowing on a set schedule. We work with body corporate committees and property managers, provide professional invoicing, and notice any maintenance issues that need attention.

Can you work with our property manager?

Absolutely. We're experienced working with property managers and body corporate contacts. We can receive instructions from your property manager, coordinate scheduling, and provide clear invoicing and communication. We understand the formal approval and payment processes that come with managing multi-unit properties.

Is battery-powered equipment suitable for commercial properties?

Yes, it's actually ideal. Battery-powered tools are quiet enough to run during business hours without disrupting tenants, office workers, or residents. No petrol fumes near building air intakes or in enclosed courtyards. Many properties value this as part of their sustainability commitments — we're eco-friendly, professional, and considerate.

How do you handle scheduling around tenants?

We coordinate with your property manager on timing. Early morning, lunchtime, or afternoon visits can be arranged to minimise disruption. Because our equipment is quiet, we have more flexibility than traditional petrol-powered contractors. We keep common areas tidy and respect tenant access and parking.
