How we do it

Fast, quiet, no mess left behind.

Green waste removal can be a standalone service or part of a larger garden project. Whether it's autumn leaves, clippings from a big prune, branches from a storm, or general garden tidying, Kai clears it all and takes it away.

We use battery-powered leaf blowers, which are significantly quieter than petrol units — that matters in residential areas where petrol-powered equipment can be disruptive and unpleasant. Battery equipment also means no exhaust fumes hanging around your property, and it's more environmentally friendly right from the start.

Leaves and green waste are collected, loaded into the van, and disposed of responsibly at approved Canberra recycling facilities — either Mugga Lane or Belconnen — where they're composted into soil conditioner rather than going to landfill. Your property ends up clean and tidy, and the waste is recycled responsibly.

FAQs

Common questions about green waste removal in Canberra.

Do you take the green waste away or leave it?

We take it with us. Once the leaves or garden waste are collected, we load them into the van and dispose of them responsibly at an approved Canberra facility. Your job is to have us come — ours is to leave your property clean and take the waste away. You shouldn't have to deal with piles of leaves or green waste after we've finished.

How much does green waste removal cost in Canberra?

Pricing depends on the volume of green waste and the size of your property. Text Kai a photo of the area that needs clearing and you'll get a clear, free quote back — usually within the hour. Most jobs are quoted as a fixed price, no surprises on the day.

Can you clear a full trailer load of garden waste?

Yes. Whether it's a couple of bags of leaves or a trailer load of branches and clippings, we can handle it. For very large jobs, we may need to arrange a second trip or discuss timing, but we're set up to clear significant volumes of green waste. Call or text photos and we'll give you a clear quote.

Where does the green waste go?

Green waste is taken to approved Canberra recycling facilities — either Mugga Lane or Belconnen recycling centre — where it's composted rather than going to landfill. The waste becomes compost and soil conditioner, so it's recycled responsibly rather than just moved to a dump. That's the eco-friendly way to handle it, and it's what we do.
