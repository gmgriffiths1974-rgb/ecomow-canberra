# Complete lawn and garden services. Quiet, clean, properly done.

From lawn mowing and edging to complete garden makeovers — ten core services across Canberra using battery-powered, solar-charged equipment. One team, tidy finishes, no drama.

Jump to a service:

## Lawn mowing and edging

Professional cuts with battery-powered equipment for a perfect finish every time. Our most requested service across Canberra — sharp, consistent and quiet enough to use on a Sunday morning without your neighbours knowing.

- Sharp, clean cuts for healthy grass growth
- Precise edging along paths and garden beds
- Quiet operation — respectful to neighbours and shift-workers
- Grass clippings mulched or removed as preferred
- Verges and nature strips included on request

## Hedging and pruning

Expert shaping and maintenance to keep your garden looking its best year-round. Whether it's a single photinia out front or a long boundary hedge that needs a full reset, we leave it neat, healthy and properly tidied up.

- Seasonal timing for optimal plant health
- Professional shaping and sizing
- Disease prevention through proper technique
- Clean-up and green waste removal included
- Quarterly maintenance plans available for big hedges

## Lawn maintenance plans

Complete lawn care programs to achieve and maintain a healthy, green lawn. Weekly, fortnightly or monthly visits — adjusted for how your lawn actually grows through the year, not a rigid template.

- Regular mowing schedules matched to growth
- Fertilising and soil improvement advice
- Weed control and prevention
- Seasonal lawn care guidance
- Per-visit or monthly billing, your choice

## Mulching

Protect your garden beds and retain moisture with professional mulch application. We use quality organic mulches laid at the right depth, kept clear of trunks and stems so your plants thrive.

- High-quality organic mulches
- Proper depth (typically 60–70mm) and coverage
- Weed suppression and moisture retention
- Enhanced soil health and appearance
- Instant visual lift for tired beds

## Garden work and cleanup

Transform overgrown areas into beautiful, manageable garden spaces. Big garden makeover jobs are our specialty — if it's been neglected for years, we love that.

- Complete garden makeovers
- Debris and green waste removal
- Planting and garden bed preparation
- One-off or ongoing maintenance
- Rubbish removal by trailer for bigger jobs

## Organic pest control

Pet-safe, organic solutions for ticks and garden pests that actually work. We prioritise low-impact, label-approved options for outdoor garden pests and only use stronger products when absolutely needed and with your approval.

- Pet and family-safe treatments
- Organic, environmentally friendly options
- Targeted tick and pest control
- Follow-up treatments as needed

## Weeding

Keep your garden beds pristine with regular weeding and prevention strategies. Hand weeding for precision, organic herbicide options only when you ask for them.

- Hand weeding for precision around delicate plants
- Organic herbicide options on request
- Mulching to prevent regrowth
- Education on weed prevention

## Gutter cleaning

Keep your gutters flowing freely with safe, thorough cleaning services. Full debris removal, downpipes checked where safely accessible, and before-and-after photos so you can see the difference.

- Complete debris removal
- Downpipe clearing and testing
- Safety-first approach with proper equipment
- Before and after photos provided

## Tree pruning and trimming

Professional tree care to maintain health, safety and appearance. Small to medium trees shaped, deadwood removed, safer walkways. We don't work in the hazard zone near live powerlines — that's a job for an Evoenergy-accredited arborist, and we'll refer you.

- Proper pruning techniques for tree health
- Safety-focused approach
- Crown shaping and deadwood removal
- Advice on tree care and maintenance
- Referrals to accredited arborists for powerline work
